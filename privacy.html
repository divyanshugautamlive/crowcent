1. Introduction

Crowcent ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is a marketing communications and business consulting firm based in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, with operations in Gurugram, Haryana, India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your personal information when you:

  • Visit our website (crowcent.com)
  • Fill out our contact form
  • Communicate with us via email or phone
  • Engage with our services
  • Interact with our marketing communications

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal data in compliance with:

  • Indian IT Act, 2000
  • Information Technology Rules, 2011 (and subsequent amendments)
  • Draft Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India's data protection framework)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (for EU residents)

Important: By accessing crowcent.com or providing us with your information, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with Crowcent, we collect information you voluntarily share:

  • Contact Form Data: Full name, email address, company/business name, phone number, service interest, message content
  • Email Communications: Messages you send to workwithgautam.live@gmail.com
  • Phone Communications: Call records and conversation notes (if you contact us)
  • Service Engagement Data: Information shared during consulting engagements, strategy discussions, and deliverables

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit crowcent.com, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP Address: Your internet protocol address
  • Browser & Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Page Interaction Data: Pages visited, scroll depth, time spent on pages
  • Referral Source: How you arrived at our website
  • Custom Cursor Tracking: Movement data for user experience optimization (anonymous, no personal identification)

2.3 Information From Future Analytics Tools

Note: We are planning to implement website analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) in the future. When activated, we will collect:

  • User behavior patterns (clicks, form submissions, conversions)
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Traffic sources and user journeys
  • Performance metrics

We will update this policy and notify you when analytics tools are implemented. You will have the option to opt-out.

5. Data Retention

Crowcent retains personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

5.1 Retention Schedule

Data Type Retention Period Reason for Retention
Contact Form Data (Inactive Leads) 1 Year Service follow-up, business development
Active Client Data Duration of engagement + 1 year Service delivery, follow-up, legal requirements
Email Communications 1 Year (or longer if contractual) Service records, dispute resolution

After the retention period ends, we securely delete or anonymize your personal data, unless:

  • We are required by law to retain it longer
  • Deletion would prevent us from fulfilling a legal obligation
  • You have an active ongoing engagement with us

6. Security Measures

Crowcent implements technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6.1 Security Practices

  • HTTPS Encryption: Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption to secure data transmitted between you and our servers
  • Secure Data Storage: Personal data is stored in secure, password-protected systems
  • Access Controls: Only authorized team members can access personal data, subject to confidentiality agreements
  • Regular Security Reviews: We periodically review our security practices
  • Incident Response Plan: In case of a data breach, we have procedures to notify affected individuals and authorities as required by law

6.2 Limitations

Important: While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. You assume some risk when transmitting personal information online.

Your Responsibility: Keep your login credentials confidential and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under Indian law and international standards, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request and obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide this information within 30 days of your request, free of charge.

7.2 Right to Correction/Rectification

If your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request that we correct or update it. We will make corrections within 15 days of verification.

7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations. We will erase your data within 30 days unless we are required to retain it for legal or contractual reasons.

7.4 Right to Withdraw Consent

If you have consented to data processing (e.g., marketing emails), you can withdraw your consent at any time. This will not affect the legality of processing before withdrawal.

7.5 Right to Data Portability (GDPR - EU Residents)

If you are an EU resident under GDPR, you have the right to request your data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another organization.

7.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain types of data processing, including direct marketing. We will honor your objection within 15 days.

7.7 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

  • Email: workwithgautam.live@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: [Your Right] - Data Protection Request
  • Include: Your name, email, specific request, and proof of identity

Response Time: We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and resolve it within 30-45 days.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

8.1 Current Cookie Usage

Currently, crowcent.com does NOT use persistent cookies or tracking pixels. However, your browser may store session data for basic functionality.

8.2 Future Cookie Implementation

We may implement cookies in the future for:

  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our site
  • Functionality Cookies: To remember your preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: To show relevant content (with your consent)

8.3 Custom Cursor Tracking (Non-Identifying)

Our website uses custom cursor tracking for enhanced user experience. This tracking:

  • Does NOT collect personally identifiable information
  • Does NOT track across websites
  • Is stored only in your browser session
  • Is used solely to improve design interaction

8.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Enable or disable cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for third-party cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8.5 Do Not Track (DNT)

If your browser sends a "Do Not Track" signal, we will respect your preference and limit tracking to essential functions only.

9.2 GDPR Compliance for EU Residents

If you are located in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements, including:

9.3 Your Consent

By providing your information, you consent to international data transfers in accordance with this policy and applicable laws.
